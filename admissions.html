Providing An Exceptional Education

Our dedicated team of admissions and financial aid professionals understands that navigating the complexities of admissions and financial aid can be overwhelming, which is why we offer personalized support every step of the way. Our goal is to ensure that every student has the information and resources they need to make informed decisions about their education and to create a pathway to success.





How To Get Started

At the University of Ghana, we serve students of all kinds, at all levels—from first years to graduate students, transitioning service members to international students, and beyond. No matter who you are or where you’re coming from, you’ll find support from our admissions counselors.
